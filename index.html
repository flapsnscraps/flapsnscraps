ABOUT ME

Craft is an old friend to me.

Hear My Story

In a family of six, I've always found myself doing things for others; for their happiness. Never has it been completely my own. Craft to me is an escape, a selfish feat.

My voice has always been meek and my head a little bowed. Craft gives me a voice. This is my identity. My independence. I'm not an influencer, nor am I a writer with crafty words. I'm a mother of two wanting to make a name as more than just the wife of my husband. If like me, you see a friend in craft, do show the world what you're made of.

Signing off,
With Love,
Flaps n' Scraps
